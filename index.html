Invest in the Future

Enrich Africa Ventures is a pre-seed fund that invests in exceptional founders primarily outside of large tech ecosystems earlier than everyone else.

Our portfolio

Our mission is to support African founders driving economic growth and social progress. We prioritize integrity, equality, and a customer-centric approach. We invest in visionary entrepreneurs shaping Africa's future.

Seed Stage Focus

We invest in start-up companies that are in the early stage. Typically, this means a company that has a scalable idea for a product or service and has created a minimum marketable product (i.e., A product or service with a small user base already)

Industries of Interest

Financial Technology, Web 3, Clean Energy, and Consumer Technology.

Geographic Focus

We invest in early-stage companies founded by young Africans globally.

Enrich Africa Ventures is a distinguished seed syndicate fund that plays a critical role in providing early-stage funding to innovative tech companies in Africa, with a specific focus on fintech, blockchain, clean energy, and consumer technology. What sets us apart is our unique network of 60+ limited partners, who are skilled operators with deep expertise in our targeted sectors. This network enables us to identify and pursue investment opportunities with unparalleled precision and efficacy. Our decentralized community approach to deal identification, evaluation, and decision-making has been a key contributor to our ongoing success, allowing us to leverage our LPs' collective insights and knowledge to navigate the complex and dynamic African tech landscape.
